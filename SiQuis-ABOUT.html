Si Quis Software

The Company
Si Quis Software enables individuals and companies to develop digital communities around ideas, products and services. We provide the foundation necessary to create meaningful on-line experiences and tap into the emerging “Passion Economy”, a new model of internet-powered entrepreneurship.

The Product
The Si Quis Software (SQS) platform is the back-end infrastructure and front-facing app individuals and companies need to tap into the new and rapidly evolving passion economy.

The Business
SQS users are able to create niche businesses of all types (verticals within horizontal markets), by creating and curating content, directories and advertising; markets to buy and sell products and services via enhanced classifieds and ecommerce; and enable real-time peer-to-peer connections.

As paradigm shifts in on-line business activity emerges, due in part to the Covid-19 pandemic and growth of the “Passion Economy”, Si Quis empowered business will have an opportunity to insert themselves into the massive revenue streams larger and well-established internet companies have largely kept for themselves.
