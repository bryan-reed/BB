Overview

The Platform

The SQS Platform is designed for small to medium sized Passion Economy businesses. SIngle or multi user capable, the platform is organized into three seperate instances accessed by user credentials and permissions.

SQS Admin
Admin is accessed by you and/or your system administrator and used to setup and maintain your site. Our easy to use admin controls enable the user to setup and maintain everything from look and feel to data structures, payment gateways, analytics and a whole lot more. Best of all, your site can be managed by you and your team without requiring assistance from the PE team at Si Quis Software.

SQS Back-End
As your Passion Business grows, its possible additional help will be required to handle your business. Customer support, billing, advertising and more are functions that need access to the day-to-day operations of the business.

SQS Front-End
The "Front-End" of the platform is you customer facing website. The culumination of settings within the Admin and management of the day-to-day data in the Back-End are presented here.
