EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

2 | OVERVIEW

Si Quis Software (Siquis.com) is a Pacific Northwest based software development company. We create a customizable framework for people to build, manage and grow “passion-based” businesses online. Virtually any type of passion-based business is possible, and virtually any number of unique websites can be built using our framework.

These passion-based businesses are verticals within horizontal markets and uniquely positioned to appeal to highly productive market segments (fiercely passionate individuals) by focusing on their core interests and commerce within these verticals. Once built, our clients have a platform to reach their audiences 24/7/365.

The emergence of the “Passion Economy” — a new model of internet-powered entrepreneurship, [citation here] is gaining momentum in both number of participants and financial opportunity. Fueled in part by a cultural shift in the way people work, a desire to create a balanced and even flow of income, the advent of digital platforms enabling online markets, and a trend towards a more focused online community experience, the passion economy is poised for rapid growth [citation here].

In contrast to the "gig economy", the passion economy is open to anyone  interested in following their passion. It's not necessary to be a creator, an  influencer, a freelancer or even a merchant. It's true, all of these amazing  people can (and do) flourish in the passion economy, but others can too.

Different from other platforms operating within the passion economy, the Si Quis Software (SQS) platform not only enables creators, influencers and gig-economy professional to participate, it enables virtually anyone who has experiences, expertise and knowledge about something they are truly passionate about and create monetizable value along the way.

3 | OUR MISSION / VISION

5 | OUR NAME

The concept of classified ads began appearing in England in the 1600s. Handwritten messages were nailed to posts by scribes and became known as “Si Quis”, a term borrowed from ancient Rome where most posted notices began with the words Si Quis — meaning "If anybody knows of…" [citation here]

Today the sheer number and variety of classifieds worldwide has grown exponentially into broad, albeit fragmented horizontal markets. Never before has creating and posting a “Si Quis” been so easy and affordable with a reach far beyond the street posts of the 17th century.  

Most importantly however, never before have individuals had the resources to pursue things they are passionate about, which brings us to back to our name… Si Quis Software.  

We've taken liberty to expand upon the definitions of "Si Quis", taking it beyond the concept of classifieds in the 1600's and associating it with the emergence of the Passion Economy of the 21st century. "If anybody knows of something related to my passions…”

6 | Market Opportunity

The Passion Economy is reinventing the way people work and the structure of our economic future. The “new owners” of this future will be many, varied and pushing an expansion of the P2P (“Passions to Profits”) paradigm. [citation here]

The market opportunity for Si Quis Software is broad, under served and supported by financial opportunity for our clients. We’ve structured our business model to couple our growth to our client’s growth across a diverse class of businesses.

We believe the Total Addressable Market (TAM) of the Passion Economy is generally comprised of four components fueling it’s potential.

TAM - Total Addressable Market

Online and Global:
Advertising: $470 B USD in 2020
Classified Advertising $22.57 B USD in 2020
E-commerce $5 T USD in 2020
Gig-economy $297 B USD in 2020
[citation here]

Passion Economy

$38 B USD in 2020


Each of these segments will contribute to the passion economy’s growth at varying degrees. In our analysis, we believe the passion economy will create incremental economic activity accross all sectors, but will disruptively migrate economic activity towards the “new owners” and away from the traditional norms of today.

Companies that have largely kept advertising revenue for themselves will see competition for these dollars as the passion economy platforms provide better access to the audiences driving these spends. [citation here] To what degree the disruptive migration takes place remains unclear. But a quick look back at the “Passion Economy”, from $0 to $38B in 2 years, would suggest a groundswell afoot. [citation here] We believe the Passion Economy TAM will eclipse $100 B by 2025.

7 | Key Features & Benefits

In general, the SQS platform enables our clients to build beautiful and focused websites that become a place for people to gather, learn, discuss and consume things they are passionate about and deeply interested in.

Our websites are built from a collection of website themes and are customizable to reflect existing brand attributes or use them “as is” to create the look and feel desired in days, not months, with no programing experience required.

The websites are structured around five key fundamentals:
Content, Resources, Forums, Classifieds and Ecommerce
Content:
Curate Content: Display and provide links to a wide assortment of blogs, news, podcasts and white-papers that are relevant to the site’s audiences. Create Content: Blogs, podcasts, news feeds, white papers. Just about anything that’s relevant to your audiences. Make it available on your site as well as available to other sites to drive traffic and revenue.

Resources:
Create curated resource pages dedicated to recognizing suppliers, products, services, websites and people that you feel your audience would be interested in. In addition, provide your suppliers with a platform to showcase, demonstrate, advertise and promote their products and services.

Forums:
Develop forums to express, share and collaborate on ideas relevant to the audience. Use our tools to manage, organize and moderate.

Classifieds:
Our full-stack CMS functionality provides back-end management of listings, user accounts, billing information, scheduling, conflict resolution, marketing and more.

Ecommerce:
Create an exciting ecommerce portal or integrate to an existing ecommerce site to further enhance the site’s user experience.

While the look and feel of these sites are unique, the infrastructure supporting them is not. Our Content Management System (CMS) provides back-end administrative functionality for our clients to autonomously manage, maintain, market and monetize their websites. In addition, our full-stack, multi-tenant architecture ensures Si Quis Software is able to efficiently and cost effectively maintain our client’s sites.
